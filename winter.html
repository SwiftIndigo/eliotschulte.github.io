Safe Winter Driving Tips

Prepare Your Vehicle

  • Check tire pressure and tread depth.
  • Ensure your vehicle’s battery is in good condition.
  • Keep windshield wiper fluid topped up and use winter wipers.
  • Inspect your brakes and lights.

Driving in Snow and Ice

  • Slow down and increase following distance.
  • Avoid sudden braking or sharp turns.
  • Use lower gears to help control your speed on slippery roads.
  • Don’t use cruise control in snowy or icy conditions.

Emergency Kit

Carry an emergency kit with essential items such as:

  • Blanket and warm clothing.
  • Non-perishable food and water.
  • First aid kit, flashlight, and extra batteries.
  • Snow shovel and ice scraper.
  • Portable phone charger.
Tip: Always check the weather forecast before traveling and consider postponing your trip if conditions are severe.
